Our Story

Chafi & Co is the result of a long and meaningful history in the gemstone world, built on a foundation of tradition and spirituality. I am md Shafi, the proud grandson of Mohammad Shafi, a famous gemologist from the 1970s. Our family has been connected to gemstones for many generations, and I am honored to carry on this tradition, named after my grandfather.

... Read more

Our family’s journey began in Rajgir, a town about 70 kilometers from Bodhgaya. My grandfather, Mohammad Safi was not only a master gemologist but was also endowed with extraordinary healing powers. During one of his travels, he visited Bodhgaya and felt the special spiritual energy of the place. After spending a few days there, he was inspired to dedicate our family’s gemstone collection to bringing happiness and healing to others. Following in my grandfather’s footsteps, my father also became a successful gemologist and healer. He was given the title of "Sufi" in our community for his wisdom and healing work. Interestingly, my mother, who joined our family after marriage, was a professional diamond cutter. Together, they believed in the power of gemstones to bring balance and positivity to people’s lives. This belief has been passed down in our family.

For many years, I worked as a supplier of gemstones internationally, sharing our family’s precious gems and their positive energy with people around the world.

Now, with Chafi & Co, we continue this special family tradition in Bodhgaya the place where it all started. Our store is more than just a place to buy gemstones—it’s a space where you can feel the spiritual and healing power that our family has cherished for generations. Each gemstone in our collection is carefully chosen for its unique energy, helping you find balance, happiness, and positivity.
