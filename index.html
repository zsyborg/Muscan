Muscan is an Indian luxury fragrance brand based in Mumbai and marketed by Chafi Fine Jewellery. Muscan stands for a personal and obstinate selection. The brand specialises in fragrance of sandalwood, Agarwood and musk. Surprises with new fragnances every season, great attention is given to the unique identity of all fragrances it make. It's name has derived from musk. Muscan brings you non alcoholic fragrances so that it can be used for worship purposes too. Complete your look with your preferred fragrance from Muscan.

Sandalwood

Sandalwood oil is extracted from the woods for use. Sandalwood is often cited as one of the most expensive woods in the world.

Musk

Musk is one of those attars that are famous all over the globe due to its calming aroma and distinct therapeutic properties.

Agarwood

Agarwood is said to be the favorite fragrance of Lord Krishna, God of Protection. The Buddha taught that the fragrance of burning Agarwood is the “scent of Nirvana” (the state of attaining Divinity).
